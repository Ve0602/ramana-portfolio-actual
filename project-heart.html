The 11 Biomarkers

Each feature was selected based on its clinical significance in cardiac failure prediction.

📅
Age
Primary risk escalator. Risk increases significantly after 55.
Risk zone: 60+
🧬
Ejection Fraction
% of blood pumped out per heartbeat. Most powerful predictor.
Critical: <40%
🧪
Serum Creatinine
Kidney function marker. Elevated = organ stress.
High risk: >1.5 mg/dL
🧂
Serum Sodium
Electrolyte balance. Low sodium indicates cardiac complications.
Concern: <135 mEq/L
⚡
CPK Enzyme
Creatine phosphokinase — elevated in cardiac muscle damage.
Elevated: >400 U/L
🩸
Platelets
Blood clotting cells — abnormal levels affect cardiac prognosis.
Normal: 150k–400k
💉
Diabetes
Metabolic comorbidity — significantly raises cardiac risk.
+18% base risk
🩺
Anaemia
Low red blood cell count — forces the heart to work harder.
+12% base risk
📈
High Blood Pressure
Hypertension — primary driver of long-term cardiac wear.
+15% base risk
🚬
Smoking
Directly damages arterial walls and reduces oxygen delivery.
+10% base risk
⚧
Sex
Clinical studies show different risk profiles by biological sex.

How It Was Built

Full Build Workflow

01
Dataset
UCI Heart Failure Clinical Records
299 patient records from Faisalabad Institute of Cardiology. 13 features across demographics, lab results, and clinical history. Target: death_event (30-day cardiac mortality).
299 patientsUCI RepositoryEDA
02
Preprocessing
StandardScaler + Stratified Split
Applied StandardScaler for feature normalization — critical for SVM performance. Stratified 80/20 train-test split to preserve class ratio. Handled outliers in creatinine and CPK with IQR capping.
from sklearn.preprocessing import StandardScaler scaler = StandardScaler() X_train_sc = scaler.fit_transform(X_train) X_test_sc = scaler.transform(X_test)
StandardScalerStratified SplitIQR Capping
03
Model
SVM with RBF Kernel — GridSearchCV Tuning
Trained SVM with Radial Basis Function kernel. GridSearchCV over C=[0.1,1,10,100] and gamma=[0.001,0.01,0.1,1] — finding optimal C=10, gamma=0.01. Achieved strong precision-recall balance critical for medical settings.
from sklearn.svm import SVC from sklearn.model_selection import GridSearchCV param_grid = {'C':[0.1,1,10,100],'gamma':[0.001,0.01,0.1,1]} svm = GridSearchCV(SVC(kernel='rbf',probability=True),param_grid,cv=5) svm.fit(X_train_sc, y_train)

Advice Engine
Risk Tier → Clinical Advice Mapping
SVM probability mapped to 4 risk tiers: Low/Moderate/High/Critical. Each tier triggers personalized lifestyle advice grounded in WHO cardiovascular guidelines — diet, exercise, medication reminders, specialist referral thresholds.
